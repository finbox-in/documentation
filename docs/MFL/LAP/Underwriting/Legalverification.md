

<h2>Legal Verification Workflow</h2>

<p>
The BCM views the un-initiated <strong>Legal verification</strong> task in the
<strong>Verifications</strong> tab and initiates it from there.
</p>

<h3>Workflow Sequence</h3>
<p>
<strong>BCM → Legal Vendor (out of scope) → State-level Legal Manager</strong>
</p>

<h3>Task Initiation and Assignment</h3>
<ul>
  <li>
    The BCM obtains the legal report from an empanelled legal vendor or lawyer
    outside the system.
  </li>
  <li>
    Uploading the legal report is <strong>mandatory</strong> while assigning
    the task.
  </li>
  <li>
    The BCM assigns the task to a state-level legal manager by selecting the
    relevant manager from a dropdown of mapped state managers.
  </li>
  <li>
    Once assigned, the task appears in the legal manager’s queue.
  </li>
</ul>

<h3>Legal Manager Review</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Review the uploaded legal report and documents</li>
  <li>Optionally upload additional documents</li>
  <li>Add remarks</li>
</ul>

<h3>Decision Options</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Recommended</strong></li>
  <li><strong>Not recommended</strong></li>
  <li>
    <strong>Hold</strong> – sends the task back to the same BCM for
    clarifications or additional documents, after which the BCM can re-assign
    it
  </li>
</ul>

<h3>Decision Rules and Visibility</h3>
<ul>
  <li>
    Once recorded, the decision <strong>cannot be edited or reversed</strong>.
  </li>
  <li>
    The legal verification task cannot be reopened.
  </li>
  <li>
    The decision does <strong>not block</strong> the overall underwriting flow.
  </li>
  <li>
    All uploaded documents remain viewable from:
    <ul>
      <li>The legal verification task</li>
      <li>
        The <strong>“Technical verification”</strong> category under the
        <strong>“All documents”</strong> tab
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>
    Task movement and remarks are visible in the
    <strong>“Timeline”</strong> sub-tab.
  </li>
</ul>
