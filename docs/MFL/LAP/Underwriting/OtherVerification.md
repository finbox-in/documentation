<h2>Other Verifications</h2>

<p>
In the <strong>“Under review”</strong> stage, the BCM can manually initiate
parallel verifications from
<strong>Verifications → “Other verifications”</strong> sub-tab.
</p>

<h3>Mandatory Parallel Verifications</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Technical verification</li>
  <li>Legal verification</li>
  <li>RCU verification</li>
</ul>

<h3>Initiation and Completion Rules</h3>
<ul>
  <li>
    Initiating all three verifications in the
    <strong>“Under review”</strong> stage is mandatory.
  </li>
  <li>
    Completion and decisioning of these verifications is
    <strong>not required</strong> during the
    <strong>“Under review”</strong> stage.
  </li>
  <li>
    All three verifications must be completed before final sanction,
    specifically at the
    <strong>“In-principle sanctioned”</strong> stage.
  </li>
</ul>
